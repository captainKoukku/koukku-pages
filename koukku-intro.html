Welcome. This page explains what Koukku is for, how we think about your data, and what makes the app different. It's linked from the app so you can return anytime.

1. What Koukku is

Koukku is a fishing companion that helps you:

  • Log catches quickly (photo, a few taps, optional voice) and keep a private diary that works offline first.
  • Log honest "no catch" outcomes as first-class events — not as a failure, but as signal. That pattern is central to how Koukku thinks about learning.
  • See your fishing on a map (spots, sessions, competitions) with privacy controls you choose.
  • Join competitions with clear rules and standings.
  • Learn and improve through feedback and — when you choose to share — anonymous comparison with other anglers.
We optimise for ease of use, fun, and learning as the three everyday cornerstones: approachable on the boat, rewarding after a trip, honest about helping you improve without turning fishing into homework.

Active session — the main "sharing is off" switch

When your active fishing session is off, nothing is shared from that session pipeline — no live position updates, no trail or area registration, no "where are they now" signal. Turn the session on when you are on the water; off when you are done.

No-catch logging in depth

Many apps only celebrate the big fish photo. Koukku treats a blank day as a real outcome — because selection bias disappears only if you also study the days you fail. The map and journal reflect effort and conditions, not only trophies. If you use nothing else in beta, try no-catch logging and tell us whether it feels fast and worth the tap.

WSI — Weather Similarity Index PROPRIETARY

WSI compares your current situation to past outcomes under similar weather conditions — not just "it is windy", but a structured similarity signal used for context-aware hints. Exact formulas stay internal so the signal stays fair and hard to game.

XP and Skill score

XP ≈ engagement and richness of your diary. Skill ≈ how well you fish relative to difficulty. The best anglers often shine when it gets tough and others blank — Skill score moves in that direction over time. The exact Skill formula is intentionally not public.

3. Federated learning and "data over ego"

Federated here means: your device and your choices stay in charge; the cloud learns from patterns many users contribute, not from exposing every individual secret.

  • Optional peer comparison is built on aggregates, not on publishing your exact spots to strangers.
  • Shared insights use k-anonymity: we only show cohort-level data when enough distinct users have opted in.
  • Location for analytics is obfuscated (grid-based or fuzzy) — your "honey hole" coordinates are not broadcast.
The deal: if you want the benefit of seeing how you compare, you contribute on fair terms. If you opt out, we don't use your data for those aggregates — and you won't see others' comparisons either. Same rules for everyone.

4. What data is collected — and what is not

✅
Account and profile Identifiers for login, display name, preferences you save.
✅
Catch and session content you create Species, measurements, notes, photos, timestamps, and location as you choose to store and sync.
⏱️
Session-gated signals — only while session is ON Live location and session-derived activity. With the session off, these signals are not sent.
🛠️
Technical / diagnostics Crash information and diagnostics where enabled, to fix bugs and improve stability.

What we do NOT do by design

  • We do not keep session-based live sharing running when your session is off.
  • We do not sell your catch diary to third parties.
  • We do not treat opting in to aggregates as permission to publish your exact spots.
  • We do not require you to stay online to log a catch.
